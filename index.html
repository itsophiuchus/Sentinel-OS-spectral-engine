Gnosticism — The Divine Spark

The material world is a prison crafted by the Demiurge. Within each being lies a divine spark, a fragment of the true God beyond the cosmos. The Great Work is the awakening of this spark, the recognition of one's true nature beyond the illusion of matter.

Shamanism — The World Tree

The shaman ascends the world tree or descends to the underworld, navigating the three realms to retrieve lost souls, commune with spirits, and restore balance. The drum is the heartbeat of the world; the journey is real.

Voudon — The Loa & The Crossroads

Spirits walk among us. Through service, rhythm, and offering, the houngan or mambo opens the gateway for the Loa to mount their horse. It is reciprocity, not worship—a contract between worlds.

Enochian—Thelema — True Will

John Dee received the language of angels. Crowley received The Book of the Law. The Aethyrs await the traveler who would do their True Will, uniting Hadit and Nuit in the dance of existence.

Chaos Magick — Paradigm Piracy

Belief is a tool, not a cage. The chaos magician shifts paradigms like masks, using whatever system produces results. Nothing is true; everything is permitted... as long as it works.

Necronomicon — The Mad Arab's Legacy

Alhazred glimpsed what lies between the stars. The Old Ones sleep but dream. To read their names is to invite their attention. The boundary between fiction and invocation dissolves.

Discordianism — Operation Mindfuck

All affirmations are true in some sense, false in some sense, meaningless in some sense. The Goddess Eris reveals that order is an illusion we impose on chaos. Fnord.

Hermeticism — The Corpus Hermeticum

Thrice-Great Hermes revealed the principles: Mentalism, Correspondence, Vibration, Polarity, Rhythm, Cause and Effect, Gender. The All is Mind; the Universe is mental.

Kabbalah — The Tree of Life

Ten sephiroth and twenty-two paths form the map of creation. The adept climbs from Malkuth to Kether, balancing the pillars of Severity and Mercy, integrating all aspects of the divine.

Rosicrucianism — The Invisible College

Christian Rosenkreutz founded the invisible brotherhood. Their manifestos promised a universal reformation of the whole wide world through the marriage of science and spirit.

Sufism — The Path of Love

The whirling dervish dissolves in divine love. Fana—the annihilation of the ego in the Beloved. Baqa—the subsistence in God. The wine of mystical intoxication reveals the unity of all.

Tantra — The Web of Energy

Shiva and Shakti unite in the bindu. The body is the temple; the senses are gates to liberation. Through mantra, yantra, and mudra, the practitioner awakens the sleeping serpent.

Taoism — The Way

The Tao that can be told is not the eternal Tao. Wu wei—action through non-action. The sage flows like water, yielding yet overcoming all. Immortality is achieved through internal alchemy.

Alchemy — The Great Work

VITRIOL: Visita Interiora Terrae Rectificando Invenies Occultum Lapidem. The philosopher's stone transforms base metal to gold, leaden soul to solar consciousness.

Gnosis — Direct Knowledge

Not faith, not belief, but direct experiential knowing. The mystic sees without eyes, hears without ears. The kingdom of heaven is within, and the keys have always been in your pocket.

Universal Mysticism — The Perennial Philosophy

All rivers flow to the same ocean. The mystics of every tradition describe the same territory in different languages. The cloud of unknowing, the dark night of the soul, the ecstatic union—all maps of one mountain.

Integration — The Stone of the Wise

The Work complete. Nigredo, Albedo, Rubedo—Dissolution, Purification, Union. The philosopher's stone is found not in the retort but in the heart of the perfected adept.
